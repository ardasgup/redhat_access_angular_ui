Priority Customer Support Routing

Red Hat Global Support Services (GSS) and Red Hat Global Partner Enablement have established Partner Escalation to enable our strategic partners to escalate unresolved or mission critical customer support cases to GSS leadership. This process will help you and Red Hat to better manage your customer's issues and provide a process for driving these issues to resolution.

Once your request is submitted, a partner escalation case will be opened with GSS and the GSS leadership team will be notified. You will then receive an email at the email address provided below with the partner escalation case number and additional information on how to track progress.
